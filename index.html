SALEM

Salem is a city on the Thirumanimuthar River, in the south Indian state of Tamil Nadu.
It is the Fifth largest urban agglomeration and metropolitan city in the state. It is famous for its steel and mango.

Namakkal

Namakkal District is a newly formed district from Salem District.
It is functioning from 01-01-1997. It consists of 8 Taluks namely Namakkal.

Kanniyakumari

>Kanniyakumari is a coastal town in the state of Tamil Nadu on India's southern tip.
Notable tourist spots include its unique sunrise and sunset points, the 41-metre (133 ft) Thiruvalluvar Statue,
and Vivekananda Rock Memorial off the coast.

Dharmapuri

Dharmapuri is a city in the north western part of Tamil Nadu, India.
It is also one of the main sericulture belts in the state.

Madurai

Madurai is an energetic, ancient city on the Vaigai River in the South Indian state of Tamil Nadu.
Millions attend the processions and ceremonies of April's Chithirai Festival celebrating Meenakshi and Lord Vishnu.

Chennai

Chennai on the Bay of Bengal in eastern India, is the capital of the state of Tamil Nadu.
The city is home to Fort St. George, built in 1644 and now a museum showcasing the city’s roots as a British military garrison

Coimbatore

Coimbatore is a city in the south Indian state of Tamil Nadu.
To the northwest is the centuries-old, Dravidian-style Arulmigu Subramaniyaswami Temple, Marudamalai.

Thanjavur

Thanjavur is a city in the south Indian state of Tamil Nadu. Its many temples include the 11th-century Brihadeeswarar Temple,
a vast Chola dynasty–era complex with a frescoed interior. Thanjavur Maratha Palace is home to the centuries-old Saraswathi Mahal Library,
with palm-leaf manuscripts, and Thanjavur Art Gallery, with bronze statues

Nilgiris

Nilgiris is a part of the Western Ghats. Ooty the “Queen of Hill Stations”,
Coonoor 19 kms from Ooty and Kotagiri 31 kms from Ooty, are the three hill stations ...
The Nilgiri Hills are part of a larger mountain chain known as the Western Ghats.

Ramanathapuram

Ramanathapuram, also known as Ramnad,
is a town and a municipality in Ramanathapuram district in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu.

Trichy

Tiruchirappalli (also called Tiruchi or Trichy) is an ancient city in India's southern Tamil Nadu state.
The Kaveri and Kollidam rivers flow around Srirangam Island, which is known for sacred Hindu sites Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple, with intricately carved gopurams (towering gateways)
